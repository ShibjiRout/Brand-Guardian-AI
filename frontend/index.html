What It Does
🎬
Video Intelligence
Extracts full speech transcript and on-screen text (OCR) from any YouTube ad via Azure Video Indexer.
🔍
RAG Compliance Check
Retrieves the most relevant brand rules from a vector knowledge base and audits content against them.
⚡
Instant Report
GPT-4o generates a structured violation report with severity levels — CRITICAL or WARNING — and a plain-English summary.
☁

How It Works
01
Submit Video
Paste a YouTube URL (auto-download) or upload your video file manually with title and description.
02
Azure Processes
Azure Video Indexer extracts the full transcript and all on-screen text (OCR) from the video.
03
RAG Retrieval
The system queries a vector store of brand compliance rules and retrieves the most relevant policies for the content.
04
GPT-4o Audit
GPT-4o cross-references the transcript and OCR against retrieved rules and returns a structured JSON violation report.
05
Results Delivered
Violations are displayed by category and severity with a plain-English summary — PASS or FAIL verdict.
Compliance Categories Checked
Misleading or absolute claims ("guaranteed", "100% safe")
Missing required disclosures (FTC, ASA, platform-specific)
Prohibited product or service claims
Video description length (recommended: ≤35 chars/line, 2 lines)
On-screen text legibility and duration
Brand tone and language guidelines
